OUR BACKGROUND

Bio Tech Energy is at the forefront of the bio-tech energy revolution. Leveraging cutting-edge biological and technological advancements, we are on a mission to redefine sustainable energy solutions. With a diverse team of experts, a history of groundbreaking innovations, and a commitment to environmental stewardship, Bio Tech Energy is driving the future of clean, renewable energy.

Bio Tech's Bio Diesel refinery is a second-generation multi-feedstock refinery, producing Bio Diesel to EN14214 standard, capable of processing waste oils with up to 100% FFA and 600ppm sulfur. The plant's production capacity of bio diesel is 45,000 Metric Tons per annum and is currently running at 90% utilization.

Being plant operators with in-house design and fabrication capacity puts Bio Tech in the unique position to be able to design, manufacture and operate complete turn key Second Generation bio diesel refineries.
